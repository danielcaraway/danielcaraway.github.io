## INDEXING & MASKING
### Masking allows you to use conditional filters to grab elements

```python
mat = np.arrange(0,100).reshape(10,10)
# get specific number
mat[5,2]
mat[:,2]
mat[2,:]
# how to mask conditionals
mat[mat>50]
```
